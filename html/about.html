ABOUT US

A & L hog meat dealer and retailer is a small business founded by Mr. Alan Lanado and his wife Mrs. Nelly Lanado , wholesaler and retailer of export-grade frozen and fresh pork products. The busIness was organized on April 30, 1992. From humble beginnings, it has grown into a strong Filipino partner of highly-reputable livestock growers and meat producers based mainly in nearby barangays of Ibaan Batangas.

Through the years, the business has managed to supply some of the market in Bicutan , Sucat, Alabang, and some areas in Pasay.

Its main customers are households and businesses that fall under Hotels, Restaurants and Institutions who purchase wholesale and retail pork products. Businesses who seek to source products from a reliable and trustworthy supplier that is consistent with their product quality, supply availability, and offers the most competitive prices.
